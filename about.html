About Us

EatBite is a restaurant locator system that was build in 2021. This system has the ability to help clients and restaurant owners in the various sectors available. Clients will have an easy time picking out the restaurant they want to dine in, in any location in the country. The restaurants are also grouped according to the different cuisines they offer. For the restaurant owners they have an option of submitting their details so that their restaurant can be listed as part of our numerous restaurants in our system.
